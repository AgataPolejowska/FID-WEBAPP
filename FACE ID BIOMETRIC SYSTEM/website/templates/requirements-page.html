FACE ID BIOMETRIC SYSTEM

PHOTO REQUIREMENTS

  1. Only one person must be in the picture
  2. The photo must clearly show the person's facial features
  3. The entire face is clearly visible
  4. The person is in focus
  5. The person must face the lens, hold head straight and look directly into the lens
  6. A neutral expresion must be maintained with closed mouth
  7. There are no dark spots or shadows
  8. The photo should not be too light or too dark
  9. The photo should not be taken from too far away or too close
